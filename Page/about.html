Welcome to Agwan Traders

Agwan Traders is a trusted electrical goods store located at First Floor, Regal Market, Karachi. Since 2010, we have been providing quality home appliances, lighting, and electrical accessories to customers across Karachi.

We specialize in products from leading brands including Dawlance, Haier, Orient, GFC, Pel, Philips, and Schneider. Our commitment to genuine products, competitive pricing, and after‑sales support has made us a preferred choice in Regal Market.

In addition to sales, we offer expert repair services for ACs, refrigerators, washing machines, TVs, and more. Our skilled technicians ensure quick and reliable fixes.
